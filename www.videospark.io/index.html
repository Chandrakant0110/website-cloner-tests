Have a question?

How does purchasing a video work?
You may purchase a standalone project or bundle directly on our site. Simply check out through Stripe, and we will instantly create your account and invite you to it.
What is the Spark System?
The Spark System is Videospark’s process integrated platform that streamlines the creative development, delivery, and analysis of video project performance. Upon purchase, you will get user access to a private customer dashboard where you can complete onboarding, communicate directly with your project coordinator, and check on the status of your project at any time.
How long does it take?
The delivery timeline is broken up into 3 phases for development and there is a review round at the end of each phase. If timely feedback is given, the process takes roughly 2 weeks from signing up to final approved video delivery.
Who actually creates my videos?
We have recruited, vetted, trained, and hired our own in-house team of creative specialists with over 20+ years of combined industry experience.
Is there a free trial available?
We do not offer a trial due to the level of customization our product requires for each customer.
What is your refund policy?
We do not offer refunds. All customers have a chance to review and agree to our Service Agreement prior to engaging with us.
